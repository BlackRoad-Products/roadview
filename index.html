01 — Features
Search That Thinks
Not just links. Verified, scored, and provenance-tracked results you can trust.
V

Verified Results

Every result is cross-referenced against multiple sources and anchored to RoadChain. You see the provenance trail — where it came from, who confirmed it, and when it was last verified. No black-box ranking.

C

Credibility Scoring

Each result carries a credibility score from 0 to 100. Agents analyze source authority, recency, citation depth, and cross-reference consistency. You decide what threshold you trust — RoadView shows the math.

R

Research Collections

Save searches into collections. Agents continue monitoring your topics in the background. When new verified results appear, your collection updates automatically. Build a living research library over time.

T

Trending Searches

See what the convoy is tracking in real time. Trending topics surface verified information first, not engagement bait. Agent-curated trends with credibility context so you know what matters and why.
